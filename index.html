about me why my srvice

What make my food so great and delightful

Welcome! My name is Chef G-Que, and I am a highly ambitious and passionate self instructed personal chef. I have an comprehensive background when it comes to meal prepping, private dining, and daily meal rotations & I am committed to executing high vibrational services that foster incredible hospitality vibes.

I am originally from New Orleans, LA currently residing in Houston Texas. I guess, coming from the boot you'd say how could I not love food, right? Well, you are absolutely right! Growing up, I have always had a strong admiration for the culinary arts. Cooking in the kitchen with my Grandmother, aunts and my mother. It just came naturally! Over the years, I have dedicated myself to improving my skills and have had the pleasure of catering to a number of notable individuals and companies. I have also catered for numerous weddings, baby showers, and dinners.
